Transhumanisme: A disturbing vision of the future

Christian Byk's book Transhumanism explores the ethical issues raised by technologies that modify the human body, and questions the future of humanity. In a world of rapidly advancing biotechnology, Byk invites us to reflect on the risks and promises of these innovations. Read his in-depth analysis the full article!

Welcome to the machine: More than a song, a reality

In Welcome to the Machine, Pink Floyd criticizes the dehumanization caused by the music industry and modern institutions. Through a dark ambience and powerful lyrics, the band denounces the alienation of the individual in a mechanized society. GATTACA - The price of perfection

In Gattaca, Andrew Niccol explores a future where genetics determine each individual's place in society, raising crucial questions about ethics and equality. The film warns of the dangers of a society based on genetic manipulation. Find out more about this dystopian vision in our full analysis!

Andrew Niccol

New Zealand director and screenwriter Andrew Niccol is well known for his dystopian works that examine the potential excesses of modern society. Films such as Gattaca and The Truman Show examine the consequences of technological control and the manipulation of reality, raising ethical questions about freedom and human identity. freedom and human identity
