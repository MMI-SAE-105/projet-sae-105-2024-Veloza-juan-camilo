HOLA !

Hello, my name is Juan Camilo Veloza and I'm a Colombian student in my first year at MMI (Métiers du multimédia de l'internet) in Montbéliard.


This site has been created to answer the “prehistory of the future” question about what our chosen objects will look like in the near future. Through different themes such as cinema, music, books and others, I will answer the riddle of how genetics will be seen in the future.


Why genetics?p>

The reason I chose the theme of genetics is that I've always liked the idea of being able to modify human beings by means of scientific elements or technologies that seem extraterrestrial, in fact it's one of the great themes of science fiction, so this subject aroused my curiosity even more.
